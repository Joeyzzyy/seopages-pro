The 10 SEO Best Practices

1

Target Long-Tail Keywords

Focus on specific queries like "Notion vs Coda for teams" rather than just "Notion alternatives". Long-tail keywords have clearer intent and less competition.

2

Optimize Title Tags

Use the format: [Product A] vs [Product B]: Best Alternative [Year]. Include the primary keyword naturally and keep under 60 characters.

3

Structure with Schema Markup

Implement FAQ, Product, and Review schema. This enables rich snippets and improves visibility in AI search results.

4

Create Scannable Comparison Tables

Use HTML tables with clear headers. Search engines extract and display table data in featured snippets.

5

Include Both Brand Names Early

Mention both products in the first 100 words, H1, and meta description. This signals relevance for both brand queries.

6

Add Fresh, Unique Content

Include original analysis, user quotes, and updated pricing. Avoid copying competitor descriptions - add your perspective.

7

Optimize for AI Search (GEO)

Write clear, factual statements that AI can cite. Use bullet points, direct answers, and structured comparisons.

8

Build Internal Links

Link to related comparison pages, your main product page, and pillar content. Create a comparison page hub.

9

Ensure Mobile Excellence

Comparison tables must scroll horizontally on mobile. Test on multiple devices and optimize Core Web Vitals.

10

Update Regularly

Set calendar reminders to update pricing, features, and claims quarterly. Add "Last Updated" dates for trust.

Keyword Research for Alternative Pages

Effective alternative page SEO starts with smart keyword research. Here's what to target:

  • "[Competitor] alternatives" - Primary target, highest volume
  • "[Your Product] vs [Competitor]" - Brand comparison queries
  • "Best [category] tools" - Category-level comparisons
  • "[Competitor] pricing" - Price-sensitive searchers

Pro Tip: According to Moz's Internal Linking Guide, pages with 3-5 contextual internal links see 40% better crawl efficiency and ranking signals.

SEO FAQ

How long should an alternative page be?

Aim for 1,500-3,000 words. Include comprehensive comparisons but avoid fluff. Quality depth beats arbitrary word counts.

Should I use competitor brand names in URLs?

Yes, for "vs" pages use formats like /your-product-vs-competitor. For "alternatives" pages, include the competitor name.

How do I get backlinks to comparison pages?

Create genuinely useful comparisons that people want to reference. Reach out to review sites, get featured in "best of" lists, and share on communities where people ask comparison questions.

Is it legal to use competitor names in SEO?

Generally yes, for honest, factual comparisons. Avoid trademark infringement (like using logos without permission) and false advertising. When in doubt, consult legal counsel.
