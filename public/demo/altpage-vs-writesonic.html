Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Seenos.ai vs Writesonic.

Seenos.ai is an SEO-first workstation with integrated GEO capabilities, currently in beta. Writesonic is an established content + GEO platform with 65+ templates, founded in 2020. Both track AI visibility, but Seenos focuses on SEO workflows while Writesonic emphasizes content generation.
Writesonic's full GEO capabilities require the Professional plan at $199/month (billed annually). Lower tiers (Lite at $39/mo, Standard at $79/mo) include basic features but limited GEO tracking. Enterprise pricing is custom. Seenos.ai is currently free during beta.
GEO is the practice of optimizing your brand's visibility in AI-powered search engines and assistants like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. It includes tracking how often your brand is mentioned, sentiment analysis, citation monitoring, and optimizing content to appear in AI-generated responses.
Seenos.ai is currently in closed beta, meaning features are still being developed and refined. It's suitable for early adopters who want to shape the product. For production-critical workflows, Writesonic offers a more mature, proven platform with enterprise compliance certifications.
Both platforms track major AI engines including ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, and others. Writesonic's GEO features are more mature with citation tracking, sentiment analysis, and competitor comparisons. Seenos.ai is building similar capabilities with an SEO-centric approach.
If you need GEO and content tools today with enterprise features, Writesonic is the safer choice. If you're SEO-focused, enjoy trying new tools, and want free beta access, join the Seenos.ai waitlist. Many users try both ‚Äî the free beta makes testing Seenos.ai risk-free.
